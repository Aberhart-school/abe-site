ACCESS:

The Attitude, Community Competence Elements {of Academic Curriculum}, Social Skills {ACCESS} program at the high school level is for moderate to severe cognitively disabled students. The guiding principle of the ACESS program is each student, in keeping with his/her individual abilities and gifts will complete the high school ACCESS program with a foundation of learning and skills to function effectively in life, work, his/her community, and continue learning and participating in leisure activities.
