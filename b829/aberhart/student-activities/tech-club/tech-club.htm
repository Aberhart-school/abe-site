For those interested in participating, we hold casual meetings on Mondays during lunch in room 310. We welcome all people from any technology field, such as Stage tech, Photography, Programming, Hardware, Software (i.e. AutoCad, Photoshop, Premiere), as well as anyone who wishes to learn! We make an active effort to pair those who want to learn with somebody who has experience at the earliest possible opportunity. We will also be holding workshops throughout the year on different aspects of technology, like helpful tips for your calculator, so keep your eyes peeled for posters near our job board. And if you didn't hear from that last line, the Technology Club has a job board too! It can be found outside room 310, and jobs posted there are open to applications from anyone in the school community. A separate stream of jobs will be offered to members only. If you still have questions, please talk to Tim, Tauran or Nathan for more details.
