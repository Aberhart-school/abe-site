Welcome to Debate! A club where you can meet friends, travel and learn how to be a better public speaker. The club meets weekly, has practices on Mondays after school and there is a tournament every month you can sign up for.

A note to parents: At least one parent is expected to attend and assist at any tournament your child signs up for, with the exception of trips
