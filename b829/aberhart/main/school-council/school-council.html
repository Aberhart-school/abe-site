The William Aberhart School Council is an advisory group that facilitates communication among all members of our School Community so that we may work together as partners. All parents (including guardians), teachers, students and other individuals in the community with a stake in William Aberhart Senior High School are members of our School Council.

Upcoming meetings

All meetings are at 7pm in the Learning Commons. Council meetings are immediately followed by the Friends of Orange meetings.
