Laptop purchase opportunity - Parent info

The CBE has been working with PC Corp and OSI, our PC and Apple vendors, to create a high value purchase opportunity for CBE students. This unprecedented opportunity offers families the opportunity to purchase either a Mac or PC laptop complete with a selection of significant educational programs such as Read and Write Gold, Comic Life and Smart Notebook. The laptop and associated programs are available at very competitive prices. Should you choose to take advantage of this opportunity, please note that this sale takes place with either PC Corp or OSI and not through the Calgary Board of Education. For further information regarding prices, application selection and ordering, please visit PC Corp or OSI at the following websites:

  • PC Corp
  • OSI

Microsoft office

Additionally, for those families who already have a computer, please be aware of an opportunity to purchase Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, and PowerPoint) for $27.50. This offer is only available through a special download site provided to the CBE by Microsoft. Your child can download a copy of Microsoft Office Student Professional and will be entitled to all future versions while a CBE student. When your child leaves the CBE, he or she retains the perpetual license for the software. Please visit this link if you are interested in ordering.
