ABE's Fall Drama Production

Auditions for ABE`s fall production are on Thursday September 8 and Friday September 9 after school in the Drama room. Students interested in being involved back stage in sound, lights, set building, costumes etc. can also sign up on those dates. The production is a memory play entitled ABERHART SUMMER by Connie Massing (an Alberta playwright).

All students are welcome! Come out to the Drama room to audition or to sign up! Please email Ms. doAmaral if you have any questions.
