Expectations

There is no food or drinks in the Library. You will be asked to leave if you are found eating in the Library. Only exception to this rule is during a C.S.I. or Guidance presentation.

Please keep the noise level down and respect your fellow students. Clean up your area before you leave and push in chairs.

The Silent Learning Area: Many who come to the library need a quiet place to study. Please respect the needs of your fellow learners and choose a different area if you wish to talk.
