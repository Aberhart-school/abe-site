ACAD's Luke Lindoe Library is a unique public institution, and its collection is open to the public year round. As Calgary's only library dedicated to art and design research and information, our collection is unique and exciting - come and check it out!

The Luke Lindoe Library staff are here to help you as you investigate topics from the Beaux Arts to biology. Come by our on-campus location to find out more about what this team of dedicated professionals can do to help you succeed in your research.

The Luke Lindoe Library supports the curriculum of ACAD. The collections are continually growing, with a focus on maintaining a current collection of publications related to art, design and emerging media. The Luke Lindoe Library has a superior collection of electronic databases, digital image banks, films + videos and e-books as well as reciprocal relationships with other institutions that allow faculty and students access to a multitude of diverse collections within the province.
