Aberhart is #1 in Canada for Blood Donations!

A special thank-you and congratulations goes out to William Aberhart High School for an amazing Young Bloods For Life Challenge. The students really rose to the challenge and engaged the whole community in their efforts to achieve first place in the nation. 729 units of blood have been donated from William Aberhart High School. The challenge brought in 127 new donors from our school alone, and our overall contributions amounted to 33% of the donations that came from Alberta.
