Aberhart to Participate in International Conference

Aberhart students will present their water research at the Water is Life Conference in the Netherlands in June 2016. Water is Life is an event that occurs every second year, uniting students from across the world to discuss water issues

The Aberhart research groups are examining water issues like drinking water quality on the T’suu T’ina Reserve in comparison to City of Calgary Residents; the current reality of the Ray Glacier and the extent to which it will continue to provide water to the Elbow River;
