ABE at the Alberta International Band Festival (1 of 3)

The William Aberhart Concert Band (our grade 10s) recently participated at the Alberta International Band Festival. There were approximately 130 bands from all over. Results as follows:

ABE at the Alberta International Band Festival (3 of 3)

The William Aberhart Symphonic Band recently participated at the Alberta International Band Festival. There were approximately 130 bands from all over. Results as follows:

  • William Aberhart Symphonic Band – Gold
  • William Aberhart Symphonic Band – Robert Eklund Trophy for Most Outstanding High School Band at the festival
  • Individual student awards – 6 awarded in the whole festival including Leslie A, Dennis K, and Adam B from Abe.
