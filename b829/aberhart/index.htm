Orange in The Netherlands and Paris

Students from Maurick College in the Netherlands visited us in April 2014 for a full French Immersion experience including our Canadian culture. Students from the French Immersion program recently visited their partners from Maurick college, experiencing life at the college and the cultural aspects that the Netherlands has to offer ( biking, eating, singing, learning Greek and Latin, etc.) We included a social studies aspect by visiting Kamp Vught and the Ann Frank museum in Amsterdam.
