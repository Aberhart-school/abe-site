Aberhart Robotics Republic

Aberhart has a new robotics team! Since September of this year, 30+ students have been hard at work to start up the Aberhart Robotics Republic. We have entered the FIRST Tech Competition, in which teams "are challenged to design, build, program, and operate robots to play a floor game in an alliance format." In addition to building and testing our competition robot, we are also actively developing multi-media content and solving standard robotics challenges.
