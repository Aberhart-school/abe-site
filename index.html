Gmail is the new student email

Students will now use Gmail for their student email accounts. Gmail is a widely used email service that allows students to work in an environment that is safe, collaborative and engaging. Gmail is part of the Google Apps for Education suite.

  • Your alias is still the same: just the domain name is changing.
  • Your old domain was @cbemail.ca. The new new domain is @educbe.ca
  • If your alias was johnd12, your Google username would be johnd12@educbe.ca

Everything else remains the same and your personal information is still protected. The links below will provide you with further information.
