To all students, with outstanding textbooks and other materials from the library:

Due to an ongoing critical shortage of textbooks in particular, we are stepping up our efforts to provide an incentive to return them. As of next Wednesday, November 13th, if you have any of these items on your account, we will be disabling your online access at Aberhart. Access will only be restored once the materials are returned or restitution arrangements made through the business office.

Thanks in advance for your prompt attention to this important issue!!
