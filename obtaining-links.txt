1 - Open Windows Explorer, navigate to root folder (b829), and search for *.pdf
2 - Select all files returned by search
3 - Hold the Shift key and Right Click on the search results
4 - Select "Copy as Path" (this option only shows up when you hold shift before the right click)
5 - Paste into a notepad window
6 - Alphabetize, remove quotes, and change backslashes to forward slashes
7 - Paste into link builder spreadsheet
8 - Copy resulting links and paste into website
